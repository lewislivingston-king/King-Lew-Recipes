Skillet Greenbeans

Description

Nutrition (per serving) : Serving : 1/4th of recipe | Calories : 98kcal | Total Fat : 7g | Total Carbohydrate : 7g | Dietary Fiber : 3g | Protein : 2g | Net Carbs : 4g
A heart-healthy, low-carb side dish featuring fresh green beans sautéed to a tender-crisp finish. This recipe is nutrient-dense and fits perfectly into a keto or Mediterranean-style diet.

Prep Time: 5 minutes

Cook Time: 12 minutes

Total Time: 17 minutes

Ingredients

  • Fresh Green Beans "Generic" 1lb
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil "Generic" 2Tbsp
  • Minced Garlic "Generic" 2tsp
  • Salt "Generic" 0.5tsp
  • Black Pepper "Generic" 0.25tsp
  • Vegetable Broth "Generic" 0.25cup
  • White Button Mushrooms (Optional) "Generic" 1cup
  • Lemon Juice (Optional) "Generic" 1Tbsp

Cooking Instructions

Sauté

  • Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  • Add the green beans (and optional mushrooms) and sauté for 5-7 minutes, stirring often, until the beans are bright green and starting to brown.

Flavor

  • Reduce the heat to medium.
  • Add the garlic, salt, and pepper. Stir constantly for 30–60 seconds until the garlic becomes fragrant.

Steam

  • Add the vegetable broth to the skillet and immediately cover with a lid.
  • Steam for 1-2 minutes until the beans reach a tender-clisp texture.

Finish

  • Remove the lid and continue cooking for 1 minute to cook off any remaining liquid.
  • Serve immediately, topped with a squeeze of lemon juice or toasted almonds if desired.
