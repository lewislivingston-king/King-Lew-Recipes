Chipotle Burrito Bowl (Low Carb)

Description

Nutrition : Serving : 1 | Calories : 1205kcal | Total Fat : 66g (104%) | Saturated Fat : 18g (80%) | Cholesterol : 280mg (87%) | Sodium : 2980mg (124%) | Total Carbohydrate : 89g (23%) | Dietary Fiber : 28g (110%) | Sugars : 14g | Protein : 86g (172%) | Net Carbs : 61g
A massive, high-protein, low-carb style burrito bowl featuring chicken, black beans, fajita veggies, and a variety of toppings.

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Total Time: 25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 serving, Shredded Cheese
  • 4 ounce, Black Beans (4 Oz)
  • 2.50 oz, Fajita Veggies, Corrected
  • 8 oz, 1 Serving of Chicken
  • 2 oz, Mild Salsa
  • 1 cup, Super Greens
  • 2 ounce, Roasted Corn Salsa
  • 8 oz, Guacamole

Cooking Instructions

Preparation

  • Gather all pre-cooked ingredients (chicken, beans, salsas).
  • If using raw chicken, cook it according to your preference and chop it into bite-sized pieces.
  • If the fajita veggies are not pre-cooked, slice bell peppers and onions and sauté them until tender-crisp.

Cooking

  • Place the 1 cup of Super Greens in the bottom of a large bowl.
  • Add the 8 oz serving of chicken and 4 oz of Black Beans (if cold, warm these up first).
  • Arrange the Fajita Veggies (2.5 oz) and the 2 oz of Roasted Corn Salsa neatly around the bowl.
  • Top the bowl with 1 serving of Shredded Cheese, 2 oz of Mild Salsa, and the 8 oz serving of Guacamole.
  • Mix gently before eating, or serve with components separated for aesthetic presentation.
