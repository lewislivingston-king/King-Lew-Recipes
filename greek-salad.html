Greek Salad

Description

Nutrition : Serving : 1 | Calories : 515kcal | Total Fat : 32g (49%) | Saturated Fat : 7g (36%) | Cholesterol : 40mg (13%) | Sodium : 2288mg (95%) | Total Carbohydrate : 47g (16%) | Dietary Fiber : 9g (38%) | Sugars : 14g | Protein : 24g (47%) | Net Carbs : 38g
A refreshing Greek salad featuring raw spinach, olives, feta cheese, and ranch dressing made with avocado oil.

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 0 minutes

Total Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 9 olives, Whole Kalamata olives
  • 28 g (1/4 cup), Fetta Cheese
  • 0.50 medium, Red onion
  • 1 cup, Florets, Raw (Broccoli/Cauliflower)
  • 1 pepper, large (3-3/4" long, 3" dia), Yellow bell pepper
  • 150 g, Spinach - Raw
  • 4 tablespoon, ranch dressing made with avocado oil
  • 9 tomatoes, Cherry Tomatoes
  • 0.50 cup, chopped, Red bell pepper
  • 2 piece, Artichokes

Cooking Instructions

Preparation

  • Wash all produce thoroughly (spinach, peppers, tomatoes, florets).
  • Chop the half medium red onion into thin slices.
  • Dice the yellow and red bell peppers into small, uniform pieces.
  • Slice the cherry tomatoes in half.
  • Drain the Kalamata olives and artichokes.

Cooking

  • In a large mixing bowl, combine the raw spinach and florets.
  • Add the chopped red onion, diced bell peppers (red and yellow), sliced cherry tomatoes, Kalamata olives, and artichoke pieces.
  • Toss all ingredients together gently until evenly mixed.
  • Crumble the feta cheese over the top of the salad.
  • Drizzle the 4 tablespoons of ranch dressing made with avocado oil over the entire salad just before serving.
  • Serve immediately.
