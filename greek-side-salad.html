Greek side salad

Description

Nutrition : Serving : 1 full recipe | Calories : 566.5kcal | Total Fat : 48.5g (62%) | Saturated Fat : 11.3g (56%) | Cholesterol : 54.8mg (18%) | Sodium : 1407.9mg (61%) | Total Carbohydrate : 22.1g (8%) | Dietary Fiber : 7.9g (28%) | Sugars : 4.4g | Protein : 14.6g | Net Carbs : 14.2g
A fresh, vegetable-forward side salad featuring spinach, feta crumbles, and Kalamata olives, tossed in a rich Avocado Oil-based Ranch dressing. High in Vitamin C (91%) and Iron (20%).

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 0 minutes

Total Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • Baby Spinach "Marketside" 3oz
  • Broccoli Florets "Marketside" 1.10oz
  • Greek Kalamata Olives, Sliced "Mezzetta" 1oz
  • Tomatoes on the Vine "Magic Sun" 1.80oz
  • Sliced White Mushrooms "Walmart" 1oz
  • Red Bell Peppers (Raw) 1oz
  • Onion (Raw) 1oz
  • Feta Crumbles "Président" 1.50oz
  • Ranch Dressing & Marinade (Made with Avocado Oil) "Sprouts" 2.50tbsp

Cooking Instructions

Preparation

  • Wash the spinach, broccoli florets, tomatoes, bell peppers, and mushrooms thoroughly.
  • Chop the tomatoes, bell peppers, and onion into bite-sized pieces.
  • Slice the white mushrooms if they are whole.

Assembly

  • In a large salad bowl, combine the baby spinach and broccoli florets as the base.
  • Add the chopped tomatoes, bell peppers, onions, and sliced mushrooms.
  • Toss in the sliced Kalamata olives and the feta crumbles.
  • Drizzle with 2.50 tablespoons of the Avocado Oil Ranch dressing.
  • Toss gently to ensure all vegetables and the spinach are evenly coated. Serve immediately.
