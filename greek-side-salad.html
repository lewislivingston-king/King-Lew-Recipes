Greek side salad

Description

Nutrition : Serving : 1 full recipe [cite: 47] | Calories : 566.5kcal [cite: 51] | Total Fat : 48.5g (62%) [cite: 57, 85] | Saturated Fat : 11.3g (56%) [cite: 57, 89] | Cholesterol : 54.8mg (18%) [cite: 57, 89] | Sodium : 1407.9mg (61%) [cite: 57, 106] | Total Carbohydrate : 22.1g (8%) | Dietary Fiber : 7.9g (28%) | Sugars : 4.4g | Protein : 14.6g [cite: 57, 90] | Net Carbs : 14.2g
A fresh, vegetable-forward side salad featuring spinach, feta crumbles, and Kalamata olives, tossed in a rich Avocado Oil-based Ranch dressing. High in Vitamin C (91%) and Iron (20%).

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 0 minutes

Total Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 oz, Marketside Baby Spinach [cite: 24]
  • 1.10 oz, Marketside Broccoli Florets [cite: 24]
  • 1 oz, Mezzetta Greek Kalamata Olives, Sliced [cite: 24]
  • 1.80 oz, Magic Sun Tomatoes on the Vine [cite: 24]
  • 1 oz, Walmart Sliced White Mushrooms [cite: 24]
  • 1 oz, Raw Red Bell Peppers [cite: 24]
  • 1 oz, Raw Onion (White, Yellow, or Red) [cite: 24]
  • 1.50 oz, President Feta Crumbles [cite: 24]
  • 2.50 tbsp, Sprouts Ranch Dressing & Marinade (Made with Avocado Oil) [cite: 24]

Cooking Instructions

Preparation

  • Wash the spinach, broccoli florets, tomatoes, bell peppers, and mushrooms thoroughly.
  • Chop the tomatoes, bell peppers, and onion into bite-sized pieces.
  • Slice the white mushrooms if they are whole.

Assembly

  • In a large salad bowl, combine the baby spinach and broccoli florets as the base.
  • Add the chopped tomatoes, bell peppers, onions, and sliced mushrooms.
  • Toss in the sliced Kalamata olives and the President feta crumbles.
  • Drizzle with 2.50 tablespoons of the Avocado Oil Ranch dressing.
  • Toss gently to ensure all vegetables and the spinach are evenly coated. Serve immediately.
