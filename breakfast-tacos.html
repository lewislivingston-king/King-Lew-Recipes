Breakfast Tacos

Description

High-protein breakfast tacos made with veggie sausage, egg substitute, fat-free cheddar, and low-carb tortillas.

Prep Time: 5 minutes

Cook Time: 10 minutes

Total Time: 15 minutes

Servings: 1

Ingredients

  • 0.50 cup Shredded Mild Cheddar Cheese
  • 1 link Veggie Sausage Link
  • 2 Zero Net Carb Carb Cutting Tortillas
  • 1 cup Egg Beater

Cooking Instructions

Preparation

  • Gather all ingredients and equipment.
  • Slice or chop the veggie sausage as desired.
  • Measure shredded cheese and have tortillas ready.

Cooking

  • Heat a nonstick skillet over medium heat and cook the veggie sausage link until browned and heated through, 3–5 minutes per side.
  • In a separate nonstick pan, add Egg Beater and scramble gently until fully set, about 2–3 minutes.
  • Warm tortillas in a dry skillet for 20–30 seconds per side or microwave between damp paper towels for 10–15 seconds.
  • Assemble tacos: divide scrambled Egg Beater and sausage among tortillas, top with shredded cheese, fold, and serve immediately. Optional: add salsa or avocado.
