Breakfast Tacos

High-protein breakfast tacos made with veggie sausage, egg substitute, fat-free cheddar, and low-carb tortillas.

Prep Time: 5 minutes

Cook Time: 10 minutes

Total Time: 15 minutes

Servings: 8

Calories: 357 kcal

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Egg Beater
  • 0.5 cup Shredded Mild Cheddar Cheese, Fat Free
  • 4 links Veggie Sausage Links
  • 2 Zero Net Carb Cutting Tortillas

Cooking Instructions

Preparation

  • Scramble Egg Beater in a nonstick pan until set.
  • Warm tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave.

Cooking

  • Cook veggie sausage links in a skillet over medium heat until browned and heated through.
  • Fill each tortilla with scrambled eggs, sausage, and shredded cheese.
  • Serve immediately. Optional: top with salsa or avocado.
