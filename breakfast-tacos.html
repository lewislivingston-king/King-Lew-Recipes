Breakfast Tacos

High-protein breakfast tacos made with veggie sausage, egg substitute, fat-free cheddar, and low-carb tortillas.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Egg Beater
  • 0.5 cup Shredded Mild Cheddar Cheese, Fat Free
  • 4 links Veggie Sausage Links
  • 2 Zero Net Carb Cutting Tortillas

Instructions

  1. Cook veggie sausage links in a skillet over medium heat until browned and heated through.
  2. Scramble Egg Beater in a nonstick pan until set.
  3. Warm tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave.
  4. Fill each tortilla with scrambled eggs, sausage, and shredded cheese.
  5. Serve immediately. Optional: top with salsa or avocado.
