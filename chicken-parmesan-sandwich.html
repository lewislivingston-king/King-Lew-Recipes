Chicken Parmesan Sandwich

Description

Nutrition : Serving : 1 | Calories : 353kcal | Total Fat : 21g (33%) | Saturated Fat : 5g (26%) | Cholesterol : 45mg (15%) | Sodium : 593mg (24%) | Total Carbohydrate : 33g (11%) | Dietary Fiber : 21g (85%) | Sugars : 1g | Protein : 24g (49%) | Net Carbs : 12g
A quick Chicken Parmesan Sandwich using a frozen patty, low-carb bun, marinara, and reduced-fat provolone cheese.

Prep Time: 2 minutes

Cook Time: 10 minutes

Total Time: 12 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 slice, reduced fat provolone deli-style cheese slices
  • 1 Bun, Hamburger Buns (Hero)
  • 1 Piece, Chicken Patties (Tyson)
  • 1 tbsp(s), Rao's Homemade Marinara Sauce

Cooking Instructions

Preparation

  • Prepare the Tyson Chicken Patty according to package instructions (e.g., microwave or oven).
  • Lightly toast the Hero Hamburger Bun, if desired.

Assembly

  • Place the cooked chicken patty on the bottom half of the bun.
  • Top the patty with 1 tablespoon of Rao's Homemade Marinara Sauce.
  • Add 1 slice of the reduced-fat provolone cheese on top of the sauce.
  • Place the top half of the bun and serve.
