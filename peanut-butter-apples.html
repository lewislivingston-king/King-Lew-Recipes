Peanut butter and apples

Description

Nutrition : Serving : 1 | Calories : 597kcal | Total Fat : 43g (66%) | Saturated Fat : 9g (45%) | Cholesterol : 0mg (0%) | Sodium : 392mg (16%) | Total Carbohydrate : 40g (13%) | Dietary Fiber : 10g (39%) | Sugars : 24g | Protein : 22g (44%) | Net Carbs : 30g
A classic, high-calorie, and high-protein snack featuring a Granny Smith apple and ground-from-fresh peanut butter.

Prep Time: 5 minutes

Cook Time: 0 minutes

Total Time: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 medium, Granny smith apple
  • 3 ounce, Peanut Butter, Ground From Fresh Peanuts

Cooking Instructions

Preparation

  • Wash the Granny Smith apple thoroughly.
  • Slice the apple into thin wedges or rings.
  • Measure out the ground-from-fresh peanut butter.

Cooking

  • Arrange the apple slices neatly on a plate.
  • Place the measured peanut butter into a small dish for dipping, or spread the peanut butter over the apple slices.
  • Serve immediately.
