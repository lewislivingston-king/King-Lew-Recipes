Turkey Quesadillas

Description

Nutrition : Serving : 1 | Calories : 310kcal | Total Fat : 5g (8%) | Saturated Fat : 0g (0%) | Cholesterol : 60mg (20%) | Sodium : 2120mg (88%) | Total Carbohydrate : 33g (11%) | Dietary Fiber : 24g (96%) | Sugars : 0g | Protein : 45g (92%) | Net Carbs : 9g
A quick and high-protein quesadilla made with low-carb tortillas, fat-free cheese, and mesquite smoked turkey breast.

Prep Time: 5 minutes

Cook Time: 5 minutes

Total Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 tortilla, Zero Net Carbs Tortilla Wraps
  • 0.50 cup, Fat Free Shredded Mild Cheddar Cheese
  • 4 oz, Turkey Breast, Mesquite Smoked

Cooking Instructions

Preparation

  • Gather ingredients.
  • If needed, slice the turkey breast into thin strips or shred the cheese.
  • Preheat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat.

Cooking

  • Lay one tortilla flat on the preheated skillet.
  • Sprinkle half of the cheese over the tortilla.
  • Layer the turkey breast evenly over the cheese.
  • Sprinkle the remaining cheese over the turkey.
  • Place the second tortilla on top.
  • Cook for 2-3 minutes per side, pressing down lightly with a spatula, until the tortillas are golden brown and the cheese is melted and gooey.
  • Slice into wedges and serve immediately.
