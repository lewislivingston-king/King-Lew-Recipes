Greek Pasta Salad

Description

Nutrition (per serving) : Serving : 1/6th of recipe | Calories : 493kcal | Total Fat : 14g (21%) | Saturated Fat : 2g (5%) | Cholesterol : 46mg (15%) | Sodium : 894mg (37%) | Total Carbohydrate : 59g (20%) | Dietary Fiber : 12g (47%) | Sugars : 7g | Protein : 29g (55%) | Net Carbs : 47g
A high-protein, high-fiber pasta salad combining whole wheat rotini, chicken breast, kidney beans, vegetables, and a creamy olive oil mayonnaise dressing. This recipe yields 6 large servings.

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 10 minutes

Total Time: 25 minutes

Ingredients (For 6 Servings)

  • 4.50 cup (56g) dry, Whole Wheat Rotini (Market Pantry)
  • 13 oz. (85g), Chicken Breast Meat (Costco - Rotisserie)
  • 1 container (1 4/5 cups ea.), Dark Red Kidney Beans Low Sodium (Target Market Pantry)
  • 1 container (2.5 cups ea.), Sugar Snap Peas (Archer Farms)
  • 1 container (2 cups ea.), Juno Bites Snacking Tomatoes (Pure Flavor)
  • 4 container (9 olives ea.), Olives to Go-kalamata Pitted Greek Olives (Pearls)
  • 2 whole, Zucchini Raw
  • 3 T, Olive Oil Mayonnaise (Hellman's)

Cooking Instructions

Preparation

  • Cook the 4.50 cups of whole wheat rotini according to package directions, then drain and cool.
  • Dice or shred the 13 oz. rotisserie chicken breast.
  • Drain and rinse the dark red kidney beans.
  • Slice the zucchini, tomatoes, and sugar snap peas.

Assembly

  • In a large bowl, combine the cooled pasta, chicken, beans, snap peas, tomatoes, sliced zucchini, and Kalamata olives.
  • In a separate small bowl, whisk the 3 T of olive oil mayonnaise with any desired liquid (like a splash of pasta water or vinegar) to thin if necessary.
  • Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently until all ingredients are coated.
  • Chill for at least 30 minutes before serving to allow flavors to blend.
