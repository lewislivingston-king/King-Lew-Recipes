Mexican Salad

Description

Nutrition : Serving : 1 | Calories : 858kcal | Total Fat : 61g (95%) | Saturated Fat : 22g (110%) | Cholesterol : 153mg (51%) | Sodium : 1011mg (42%) | Total Carbohydrate : 38g (13%) | Dietary Fiber : 14g (56%) | Sugars : 7g | Protein : 47g (94%) | Net Carbs : 24g
A high-protein, modified Mexican salad with lean ground beef, fresh cilantro, black beans, and a creamy avocado oil ranch dressing.

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Total Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 0.50 Avocado, Large Avocado 200g
  • 2 fluid ounce, Spinach - Raw
  • 4 ounce, Lean Ground Beef
  • 1 ounce, Organic Cilantro
  • 0.50 cup (28g), Gouda Shredded Cheese
  • 2 tbsp, Ranch Dressing & Marinade with Avocado Oil
  • 0.50 Tomato (148g), Roma Tomato
  • 0.25 cup, Black beans - 15.5 oz can
  • 0.25 cup, Can Corn
  • 15 gram, sliced Greek katamala olives
  • 2 tbsp(s), Sour cream

Cooking Instructions

Preparation

  • Wash and chop the cilantro, spinach, and Roma tomato.
  • Drain and rinse the black beans and corn.
  • Dice the avocado and slice the olives.

Cooking

  • In a large skillet, brown the 4 oz of Lean Ground Beef. Drain any excess fat.
  • Season the ground beef with your favorite Mexican spices (optional).
  • In a large bowl, create a base with the 2 fluid oz of raw spinach.
  • Add the cooked ground beef, diced Roma tomato, black beans, corn, and sliced olives to the bowl.
  • Top with the Gouda shredded cheese and diced avocado.
  • Drizzle with the Ranch Dressing and top with the fresh cilantro and sour cream.
  • Toss gently or serve layered.
