Almond butter and apples

Description

Nutrition : Serving : 1 | Calories : 350kcal | Total Fat : 24g (37%) | Saturated Fat : 2g (8%) | Cholesterol : 0mg (0%) | Sodium : 0mg (0%) | Total Carbohydrate : 31g (10%) | Dietary Fiber : 11g (44%) | Sugars : 19g | Protein : 11g (21%) | Net Carbs : 20g
A quick, high-fiber, and high-protein snack featuring Granny Smith apple slices and roasted almond butter.

Prep Time: 5 minutes

Cook Time: 0 minutes

Total Time: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp(s), Roasted Almond Butter
  • 1 Medium, Granny Smith Apple

Cooking Instructions

Preparation

  • Wash the Granny Smith apple thoroughly.
  • Slice the apple into thin wedges or rings.
  • Measure out the roasted almond butter.

Cooking

  • Arrange the apple slices neatly on a plate.
  • Place the measured almond butter into a small dish for dipping, or drizzle/spread the almond butter over the apple slices.
  • Serve immediately.
