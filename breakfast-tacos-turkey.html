Breakfast Tacos Turkey

Description

Nutrition : Serving : 1 | Calories : 400kcal | Total Fat : 17g (26%) | Saturated Fat : 8g (38%) | Cholesterol : 105mg (35%) | Sodium : 845mg (35%) | Total Carbohydrate : 13g (4%) | Dietary Fiber : 10g (40%) | Sugars : 1g | Protein : 48g (92%) | Net Carbs : 3g
A high-protein, low-carb breakfast featuring seasoned ground turkey, egg substitute, shredded cheese, and salsa, all wrapped in zero-carb tortillas.

Prep Time: 5 minutes

Cook Time: 10 minutes

Total Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 tortilla, zero carb tortilla (Mission Artisan)
  • 4 oz, Ground Turkey (Jenny O Turkey)
  • 0.75 cup, Egg Substitute (Egg Beater Original)
  • 0.25 cup, Fiesta Blend Shredded Cheese (Kroger)
  • 2 tbsp Salsa (Original, Medium) (Archer Farms)

Cooking Instructions

Preparation

  • Brown the 4 oz of Ground Turkey in a skillet.
  • Season the turkey and drain any excess liquid.
  • Warm the 2 zero-carb tortillas according to package directions.

Cooking

  • Pour the 0.75 cup of Egg Substitute into the skillet with the turkey and scramble until fully cooked.
  • Stir in the 0.25 cup of Fiesta Blend Shredded Cheese until melted.
  • Divide the turkey and egg mixture evenly between the two warmed tortillas.
  • Top with 2 tablespoons of Salsa and serve immediately.
