Chicken Fajitas

Description

Nutrition : Serving : 5 | Calories : 402kcal | Total Fat : 10g (16%) | Saturated Fat : 0g (0%) | Cholesterol : 53mg (18%) | Sodium : 1766mg (74%) | Total Carbohydrate : 43g (14%) | Dietary Fiber : 26g (103%) | Sugars : 4g | Protein : 38g (77%) | Net Carbs : 17g
A quick-prep chicken fajitas recipe using low-carb tortillas, lots of fresh peppers, and reduced-fat cheese.

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 20 minutes

Total Time: 35 minutes

Ingredients

  • 16 oz, Chicken Fajitas
  • 1 pepper, Bell Pepper, Red
  • 10 tbsp, Salsa (Original, Medium)
  • 1 Pepper, Green Bell Pepper, Large, Grilled
  • 1 container (32 tbsp ea.), Pico De Gallo W/ Cilantro
  • 0.50 large, Red Onion (1 Large)
  • 10 Tortilla, Carb Counter Zero Net Carbs Soft Taco
  • 1 bell pepper, Large Orange Bell Pepper
  • 1.25 cup, Colby Jack Reduced Fat Shredded Cheese

Cooking Instructions

Preparation

  • Slice the red onion, red bell pepper, and large orange bell pepper into thin strips.
  • If using a raw green bell pepper, slice it as well.
  • Cut the 16 oz of chicken fajita meat into thin strips if not already prepared.

Cooking

  • Heat a large skillet or griddle over medium-high heat.
  • Cook the chicken fajita meat until browned and cooked through. Remove from the skillet.
  • In the same skillet, add the sliced bell peppers and red onion. Sauté for 5-7 minutes until tender-crisp.
  • Return the chicken to the skillet with the vegetables. Mix well.
  • Serve by warming the 10 Carb Counter Tortillas.
  • Fill each tortilla with the chicken and vegetable mixture. Top with 1.25 cup Colby Jack Reduced Fat Shredded Cheese, 10 tbsp Salsa, and the container of Pico De Gallo.
