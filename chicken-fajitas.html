Chicken Fajitas

Description

Nutrition : Serving : 5 | Calories : 402kcal | Total Fat : 10g (16%) | Saturated Fat : 0g (0%) | Cholesterol : 53mg (18%) | Sodium : 1766mg (74%) | Total Carbohydrate : 43g (14%) | Dietary Fiber : 26g (103%) | Sugars : 4g | Protein : 38g (77%) | Net Carbs : 17g
A chicken fajita recipe using low-carb tortillas and a mix of chicken fajita strips, bell peppers, pico de gallo, salsa, and reduced-fat cheese. Note: This recipe yields 5 servings.

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Total Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 16 oz, Chicken Fajitas (John Soules Food)
  • 1 pepper, Bell Pepper, Red (Raw Red Pepper)
  • 1 bell pepper, Large Orange Bell Pepper (Generic)
  • 1 Pepper, Green Bell Pepper, Large, Grilled (Grilled Pepper - Net Carbs)
  • 0.50 large, Red Onion (1 Large)
  • 10 Tortilla Carb Counter Zero Net Carbs Soft Taco (La Banderita)
  • 1.25 cup, Colby Jack Reduced Fat Shredded Cheese (Kroger)
  • 10 tbsp Salsa (Original, Medium) (Archer Farms)
  • 1 container (32 tbsp ea.) Pico De Gallo W/ Cilantro (Safeway Select)

Cooking Instructions

Preparation

  • Slice the red, orange, and green bell peppers and the red onion.
  • Heat the 16 oz of prepared chicken fajitas.

Cooking

  • In a large skillet, sauté the sliced peppers and onion until tender-crisp.
  • Combine the vegetables with the heated chicken fajitas.
  • Warm the 10 zero net carb tortillas according to package directions.

Assembly

  • Fill each tortilla with the chicken and vegetable mixture.
  • Top with 1.25 cup of Colby Jack Reduced Fat Shredded Cheese, 10 tbsp of Salsa, and a serving of Pico De Gallo.
