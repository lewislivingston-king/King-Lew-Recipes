Taco Salad (Chicken)

Description

Nutrition (per serving) : Serving : 1.0 | Calories : 1062 kcal | Total Fat : 85 g (131%) | Saturated Fat : 15 g (75%) | Polyunsaturated Fat : 25 g | Monounsaturated Fat : 40 g | Trans Fat : 0 g | Cholesterol : 180 mg (60%) | Sodium : 2085 mg (87%) | Potassium : 1615 mg (46%) | Total Carbohydrate : 40 g (13%) | Dietary Fiber : 14 g (56%) | Sugars : 6 g | Protein : 90 g (180%) | Vitamin A : 141% | Vitamin C : 92% | Calcium : 19% | Iron : 48%
A high-protein, satisfying salad loaded with chicken, avocado, vegetables, cheese, and crunchy protein chips.

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 0 minutes

Total Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients (For 1 Serving)

  • Organic Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Kirkland, 1 tablespoon
  • Spinach, raw, fresh, (N/A), 2 oz
  • Fresh, Lime Juice, (N/A), 1 tbsp(s)
  • Low Sodium Taco Seasoning, (N/A), 1.50 tbsp
  • Avocado Oil Ranch Dressing, Primal Kitchen, 2 tablespoon
  • Fat Free Cheddar, Kraft, 28 gram
  • Water, (N/A), 2 tbsp(s)
  • Tortilla Style Protein Chips, Nacho Cheese, Quest, 1 bag
  • Green onions, chopped, (N/A), 0.33 cup
  • Grape tomatoes, (N/A), 0.50 cup
  • Rotisserie Chicken Breast Meat, Costco, 8 oz
  • Hass Avocado - Small, (N/A), 1 Avocado
  • Fresh, Usda, Cilantro, (N/A), 2 Tbsp
  • Sliced Olives (Black), Great Value, 0.25 cup(s)

Cooking Instructions

  1. **Prep Chicken:** If the 8 oz of Costco Rotisserie Chicken Breast Meat is whole, shred or chop it into bite-sized pieces.
  2. **Make Taco Seasoning Paste:** In a small bowl, combine the 1.50 tbsp of Low Sodium Taco Seasoning with the 2 tbsp of Water and 1 tbsp of Fresh, Lime Juice to form a thick paste. Mix the shredded chicken with this seasoning paste to coat evenly.
  3. **Prepare Vegetables and Toppings:** Slice the 0.33 cup of Green onions and 0.50 cup of Grape tomatoes. Halve the 1 Avocado and dice it. Roughly chop the 2 Tbsp of Cilantro.
  4. **Assemble Salad Base:** In a large bowl, combine the 2 oz of Spinach, raw, fresh, the seasoned chicken, the diced avocado, the chopped green onions, grape tomatoes, and 0.25 cup(s) of Sliced Olives (Black).
  5. **Add Dressing and Cheese:** Drizzle the salad mixture with 2 tablespoon of Avocado Oil Ranch Dressing and toss lightly. Top with 28 gram of Fat Free Cheddar.
  6. **Serve:** Serve the salad immediately, crushing the 1 bag of Tortilla Style Protein Chips, Nacho Cheese over the top just before eating for crunch, and garnish with fresh cilantro.
