Taco Salad (Chicken)

Description

Nutrition (per serving) : Serving : 1.0 | Calories : 1062 kcal | Total Fat : 85 g (131%) | Saturated Fat : 15 g (75%) | Polyunsaturated Fat : 25 g | Monounsaturated Fat : 40 g | Trans Fat : 0 g | Cholesterol : 180 mg (60%) | Sodium : 2085 mg (87%) | Potassium : 1615 mg (46%) | Total Carbohydrate : 40 g (13%) | Dietary Fiber : 14 g (56%) | Sugars : 6 g | Protein : 90 g (180%) | Vitamin A : 141% | Vitamin C : 92% | Calcium : 19% | Iron : 48%
A high-protein, satisfying salad loaded with pre-cooked chicken, avocado, vegetables, cheese, and crunchy protein chips.

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 0 minutes

Total Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients (For 1 Serving)

  • Organic Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Kirkland, 1 tablespoon
  • Spinach, raw, fresh, (N/A), 2 oz
  • Fresh, Lime Juice, (N/A), 1 tbsp(s)
  • Low Sodium Taco Seasoning, (N/A), 1.50 tbsp
  • Avocado Oil Ranch Dressing, Primal Kitchen, 2 tablespoon
  • Fat Free Cheddar, Kraft, 28 gram
  • Water, (N/A), 2 tbsp(s)
  • Tortilla Style Protein Chips, Nacho Cheese, Quest, 1 bag
  • Green onions, chopped, (N/A), 0.33 cup
  • Grape tomatoes, (N/A), 0.50 cup
  • Rotisserie Chicken Breast Meat, Costco, 8 oz
  • Hass Avocado - Small, (N/A), 1 Avocado
  • Fresh, Usda, Cilantro, (N/A), 2 Tbsp
  • Sliced Olives (Black), Great Value, 0.25 cup(s)

Cooking Instructions

Preparation (15 minutes)

  1. If the chicken breast meat is whole, shred or chop it into bite-sized pieces.
  2. In a small bowl, combine the Low Sodium Taco Seasoning with the Water and Lime Juice to form a thick seasoning paste.
  3. Mix the shredded chicken with the seasoning paste to coat evenly.
  4. Slice the Green onions and Grape tomatoes. Dice the Avocado and roughly chop the Cilantro.

Cooking (0 minutes)

No cooking is required for this recipe as it uses pre-cooked rotisserie chicken.

Assembly

  1. In a large bowl, combine the Spinach, seasoned chicken, diced avocado, chopped green onions, grape tomatoes, and Sliced Olives.
  2. Drizzle the salad mixture with Avocado Oil Ranch Dressing and toss lightly.
  3. Top the salad with Fat Free Cheddar.
  4. Serve the salad immediately, crushing the Tortilla Style Protein Chips over the top just before eating for crunch, and garnish with fresh cilantro.
