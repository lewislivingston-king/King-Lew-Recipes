Gumbo

Description

Nutrition (per serving) : Serving : 1/8th of recipe | Calories : 396kcal | Total Fat : 9g (14%) | Saturated Fat : 2g (10%) | Cholesterol : 84mg (28%) | Sodium : 2026mg (84%) | Total Carbohydrate : 51g (17%) | Dietary Fiber : 5g (22%) | Sugars : 6g | Protein : 31g (61%) | Net Carbs : 46g
A hearty, multi-protein Gumbo using a Cajun mix base, loaded with chicken, sausage, shrimp, crab, and now featuring the "Holy Trinity" (onion, celery) and okra for thickening.

Prep Time: 20 minutes

Cook Time: 45 minutes

Total Time: 1 hour 5 minutes

Ingredients (For 8 Servings)

  • 1 tbsp, Olive Oil (For Sautéing)
  • 1 large, Yellow Onion, chopped
  • 2 stalks, Celery, chopped
  • 1 cup, Frozen Okra, sliced
  • 10 cup, prepared per directions Cajun Gumbo Mix
  • 14 oz, Chicken Hardwood Smoked Sausage
  • 16 oz., Grilled Chicken Breast - Skinless, Boneless
  • 4 oz, Large Tail Off Shrimp 41-50
  • 1.50 cup, Crab Delights Chunk Style
  • 3.50 cup, Organic Stewed Tomatoes Italian Style Sliced, Roma Tomatoes
  • 6 cup cooked, Brown Rice (Boil Bag)

Cooking Instructions

Sautéing the Trinity

  1. Heat the 1 tbsp of olive oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped **Yellow Onion** and **Celery** and sauté for about 5-7 minutes until the onions are translucent.

Assembly & Simmering

  1. Prepare the 10 cups of Cajun Gumbo Mix according to its package directions.
  2. Add the cooked Gumbo liquid and the 3.50 cups of Stewed Tomatoes to the pot.
  3. Add the sliced **Frozen Okra**, 14 oz of Chicken Sausage, 16 oz of Grilled Chicken, and 1.50 cups of Crab Delights. Bring to a low boil, then reduce heat and simmer for at least 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. In the last 5-10 minutes of simmering, add the 4 oz of Shrimp. Cook until the shrimp are pink and opaque.
  5. Serve the Gumbo over the 6 cups of cooked Brown Rice.
