A high-protein, seafood and poultry gumbo featuring Cajun gumbo mix, chicken sausage, shrimp, and brown rice. This recipe makes 8 servings.

Prep Time: 20 minutes

Cook Time: 45 minutes

Total Time: 1 hour 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1.50 cup, Crab Delights Chunk Style (Louis Kemp)
  • 14 oz, Chicken Hardwood Smoked Sausage (Hills Hire Farm)
  • 16 oz, Grilled Chicken Breast - Skinless, Boneless (Johns)
  • 4 oz, Large Tail Off Shrimp 41-50 (Market Pantry)
  • 6 cup cooked, Brown Rice (Boil Bag) (Uncle Ben's)
  • 10 cup, prepared per directions Cajun Gumbo Mix (Louisiana Fish Fry Products)
  • 3.50 cup, Organic Stewed Tomatoes Italian Style Sliced, Roma Tomatoes (Kirkland)

Cooking Instructions

Preparation

  • Prepare the Cajun Gumbo Mix according to package directions, setting aside the 10 cups of prepared broth.
  • Cook the 6 cups of brown rice per package directions (boil bag) and set aside.
  • Slice the chicken sausage into coins. Chop the grilled chicken breast into bite-sized pieces.
  • Thaw and peel the shrimp, if necessary.

Cooking

  • In a large Dutch oven or stock pot, brown the sliced chicken sausage. Remove and set aside.
  • Add the 10 cups of prepared gumbo mix base and the 3.50 cups of organic stewed tomatoes to the pot. Bring to a simmer.
  • Add the cooked chicken sausage and grilled chicken breast to the simmering mixture.
  • Stir in the 1.50 cup of Crab Delights.
  • Continue to simmer for 15 minutes to allow flavors to meld.
  • Add the 4 oz of shrimp and cook for 3-5 minutes, until the shrimp are pink and cooked through.
  • Serve the gumbo over the 6 cups of cooked brown rice.
