Taco Salad (Beef)

Description

Nutrition (per serving) : Serving : 1.0 | Calories : 1169 kcal | Total Fat : 80 g (123%) | Total Carbohydrate : 40 g | Dietary Fiber : 14 g | Net Carbs : 26 g | Protein : 78 g (156%)
A flavorful, high-protein taco salad featuring seasoned lean ground beef, fresh vegetables, and crunchy protein chips.

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 10 minutes

Total Time: 25 minutes

Ingredients (For 1 Serving)

  • Fat Free Cheddar, (N/A), 28 gram
  • Organic Extra Virgin Olive Oil, (N/A), 1 tablespoon
  • Low Sodium Taco Seasoning, (N/A), 1.50 tbsp
  • Tortilla Style Protein Chips, Nacho Cheese, (N/A), 1 bag
  • Spinach, raw, fresh, (N/A), 2 oz
  • Sliced Olives (Black), (N/A), 0.25 cup(s)
  • Fresh, Cilantro, (N/A), 2 Tbsp
  • Fresh, Lime Juice, (N/A), 1 tbsp(s)
  • Hass Avocado - Small, (N/A), 1 Avocado
  • Avocado Oil Ranch Dressing, (N/A), 2 tablespoon
  • Green onions, chopped, (N/A), 0.33 cup
  • Ground Sirloin, 90% lean, (N/A), 8 oz
  • Grape tomatoes, (N/A), 0.50 cup
  • Water, (N/A), 2 tbsp(s)

Cooking Instructions

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of Organic Extra Virgin Olive Oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add the 8 oz of Ground Sirloin and brown the beef, breaking it up as it cooks. Drain any excess grease.
  2. Once cooked, stir in the 1.50 tbsp of Low Sodium Taco Seasoning, 2 tbsp of Water, and 1 tbsp of Fresh, Lime Juice. Cook for 1-2 minutes until the liquid is absorbed and the beef is well-coated. Set aside.
  3. Slice the 0.33 cup of Green onions, 0.50 cup of Grape tomatoes, and roughly chop the 2 Tbsp of Fresh, Cilantro. Dice the 1 Avocado.
  4. In a large bowl, combine the 2 oz of Spinach, raw, fresh, the seasoned beef, the diced avocado, the chopped green onions, grape tomatoes, and 0.25 cup(s) of Sliced Olives (Black).
  5. Drizzle the salad mixture with 2 tablespoon of Avocado Oil Ranch Dressing and toss lightly. Top with 28 gram of Fat Free Cheddar.
  6. Serve the salad immediately, crushing the 1 bag of Tortilla Style Protein Chips, Nacho Cheese over the top for crunch.
