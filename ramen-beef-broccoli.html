Spicy Beef & Broccoli Ramen

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef (85/15) "Generic" 1.5 lbs
  • Broccoli Florets "Generic" 4 cups
  • Spicy Beef Flavor Egg White Ramen Noodles "Miracle Noodle" 2 packages
  • Sugar Free Stir Fry Sauce "G Hughes" 6 oz
  • Avocado Oil "Generic" 1 Tbsp
  • Garlic (minced) "Generic" 2 cloves
  • Ginger (minced) "Generic" 1 tsp
  • Green Onions "Generic" 2 stalks

Cooking Instructions

  1. Rinse and drain "Miracle Noodle" ramen noodles thoroughly.
  2. Brown ground beef in avocado oil over medium-high heat.
  3. Sauté broccoli with the beef until tender-crisp.
  4. Stir in garlic, ginger, and "G Hughes" sauce, cooking for 1 minute until fragrant.
  5. Incorporate noodles, heating through for 3 minutes.
  6. Garnish with sliced green onions and serve.
