Chipotle Dressing (Homemade/Low Carb)

Description

Nutrition (per serving) : Serving : 1/5th of recipe | Calories : 222 kcal | Total Fat : 22 g (34%) | Total Carbohydrate : 3 g (Total Carbs for 1/5th) | Dietary Fiber : 1 g (Total Fiber for 1/5th) | Net Carbs : 2 g | Protein : 0 g
A flavorful, low-carb chipotle dressing using a keto honey substitute to keep the net carbohydrate count very low.

Prep Time: 5 minutes

Cook Time: 0 minutes

Total Time: 5 minutes

Ingredients (For 5 Servings)

  • Vinegar, (N/A), 6 Tablespoon
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil, (N/A), 0.50 cup(s)
  • Garlic, (N/A), 2 tsp
  • Chipotle Peppers In Adobo Sauce, Goya, 2 tbsp (34g)
  • Spices, oregano, dried, (N/A), 0.50 tsp (1.8 g)
  • Ground Black Pepper, (N/A), 0.50 tsp
  • Keto Honey Substitute, Wholesome Yum Zero Sugar Honey Substitute, 3 tbsp
  • Salt, (N/A), 0.75 tsp

Cooking Instructions

  1. Measure all liquid and solid ingredients.
  2. Carefully remove 2 tbsp of Chipotle Peppers in Adobo Sauce and place them into a food processor or blender.
  3. Add the vinegar, Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Keto Honey Substitute, garlic, dried oregano, salt, and ground black pepper to the blender.
  4. Blend on high speed for 30–60 seconds, or until the dressing is completely smooth and the peppers are fully incorporated.
  5. Taste and adjust salt or vinegar if needed. Serve immediately, or store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to one week.
