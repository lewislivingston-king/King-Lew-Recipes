Chipotle Dressing HM

Description

Nutrition : Serving : 5 | Calories : 222kcal | Total Fat : 22g (34%) | Saturated Fat : 3g (16%) | Cholesterol : 0mg (0%) | Sodium : 397mg (17%) | Total Carbohydrate : 14g (5%) | Dietary Fiber : 4g (17%) | Sugars : 4g | Protein : 0g (1%) | Net Carbs : 10g
A homemade chipotle dressing featuring extra virgin olive oil, spices, and a keto honey substitute.

Prep Time: 5 minutes

Cook Time: 0 minutes

Total Time: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 6 Tablespoon, Vinegar
  • 0.50 cup(s), Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 2 tsp, Garlic
  • 2 tbsp (34g), Goya
  • 0.50 tsp (1.8 g), Spices, oregano, dried
  • 0.50 tsp, Ground Black Pepper
  • 3 tbsp, Keto Honey Substitute
  • 0.75 tsp, Salt

Cooking Instructions

Preparation

  • Gather all ingredients and measuring tools.
  • Mince or finely chop the garlic.
  • Measure out all liquid and dry ingredients.

Cooking

  • In a medium bowl or a jar with a tight-fitting lid, combine the vinegar, Extra Virgin Olive Oil, minced garlic, Goya, dried oregano, Ground Black Pepper, Keto Honey Substitute, and Salt.
  • If using a bowl, whisk vigorously until the dressing is well-emulsified (combined).
  • If using a jar, cover tightly and shake vigorously for 30-60 seconds until emulsified.
  • Taste and adjust seasoning if necessary (add more salt or pepper to taste).
  • Serve immediately or store in the refrigerator.
