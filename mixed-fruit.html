Mixed Fruit

Description

Nutrition : Serving : 4 | Calories : 182kcal | Total Fat : 1g (2%) | Cholesterol : 0mg (0%) | Sodium : 10mg (0%) | Total Carbohydrate : 44g (15%) | Dietary Fiber : 12g (50%) | Sugars : 29g | Protein : 4g (7%) | Net Carbs : 32g
A large, high-fiber mixed fruit salad featuring mandarin oranges, blackberries, raspberries, blueberries, and strawberries. Note: This recipe yields 4 servings.

Prep Time: 5 minutes

Cook Time: 0 minutes

Total Time: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 cup, Canned Mandarin Oranges Drained
  • 1 pint as purchased, yields, Raspberries - Raw
  • 0.75 cup, halves, Strawberries - Raw
  • 200 g, Blackberries - Raw
  • 1 pint as purchased, yields, Blueberries - Raw

Cooking Instructions

Preparation

  • Gently wash all the raw berries.
  • Drain the canned mandarin oranges.

Assembly

  • In a large bowl, combine the drained mandarin oranges, raw raspberries, strawberries, blackberries, and blueberries.
  • Toss gently to mix.
  • Divide into four equal servings and chill before serving.
