Salmon & Pearl Couscous

Description

Nutrition : Serving : 1 | Calories : 447kcal | Total Fat : 21g (33%) | Saturated Fat : 6g (38%) | Cholesterol : 113mg (38%) | Sodium : 261mg (11%) | Total Carbohydrate : 27g (9%) | Dietary Fiber : 2g (7%) | Sugars : 0g | Protein : 34g (68%) | Net Carbs : 25g
A high-protein meal featuring 6 oz of cooked Salmon Milano served with 0.75 cup of cooked pearl couscous.

Prep Time: 5 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Total Time: 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 0.75 cup, cooked Couscous, cooked
  • 6 oz, Salmon Milano (Costco)

Cooking Instructions

Preparation

  • Prepare the 0.75 cup of couscous according to package directions.
  • Cook the 6 oz Salmon Milano using your preferred method (e.g., pan-sear, bake, or microwave).

Assembly

  • Place the cooked couscous on a plate or in a meal prep container.
  • Top the couscous with the cooked Salmon Milano and serve warm.
