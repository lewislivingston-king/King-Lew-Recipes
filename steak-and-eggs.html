A high-protein, low-carb breakfast featuring lean beef fajita meat and Egg Beaters Original.

Prep Time: 5 minutes

Cook Time: 10 minutes

Total Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup, Egg Beaters Original Real Egg Product
  • 2 oz, Beef Fajita

Cooking Instructions

Preparation

  • Gather all ingredients.
  • Ensure the beef fajita meat is thawed and cut into bite-sized pieces if needed.
  • Pre-heat a skillet over medium-high heat with a small amount of cooking spray or oil.

Cooking

  • Add the beef fajita meat to the hot skillet. Cook for 5-7 minutes until browned and cooked through. Remove from the skillet and set aside, keeping warm.
  • Pour the 1 cup of Egg Beaters into the same skillet.
  • Scramble the eggs, stirring occasionally, until they are fully set (about 3-4 minutes).
  • Return the cooked beef to the skillet or serve immediately on the side of the scrambled eggs.
  • Season with salt and pepper to taste.
