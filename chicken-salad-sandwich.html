Chicken Salad Sandwich

Servings: 4 • Calories per serving: 393 kcal

Ingredients

  • 3 large egg whites (57g each)
  • 4 classic burger buns
  • 3 stalks celery (11"–12" long)
  • 16.5 oz hand-pulled rotisserie chicken breast
  • 5 tbsp olive oil mayo
  • 2 tbsp dill relish

Preparation

  1. Finely chop celery and egg whites. Shred rotisserie chicken if not already pulled.
  2. In a large bowl, combine chicken, celery, egg whites, mayo, and dill relish. Mix until evenly coated.
  3. Taste and adjust seasoning if desired (optional: add salt, pepper, or mustard).
  4. Spoon mixture onto burger buns and serve immediately, or chill for 30 minutes before serving.
