Crab Salad Sandwich (copy)

Description

Nutrition : Serving : 4 | Calories : 334kcal (17%) | Total Fat : 16g (24%) | Saturated Fat : 2g (9%) | Cholesterol : 28mg (9%) | Sodium : 1144mg (48%) | Total Carbohydrate : 44g (15%) | Dietary Fiber : 23g (91%) | Sugars : 1g | Protein : 21g (42%) | Net Carbs : 21g
A classic sandwich featuring large egg whites, classic white bread, Crab Classic, mayonnaise, celery, and dill relish.

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 0 minutes

Total Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 egg (57g), Large Egg White
  • 8 slice, Classic White Bread
  • 16.60 oz(s), Crab Classic
  • 4 tbsp, Classic Mayo
  • 3 stalk large (11"-12" long), Celery - Raw
  • 2 tbsp, Dill Relish *correct - Cdn Bog

Cooking Instructions

Preparation

  • Hard-boil the egg whites, if not already prepared.
  • Finely chop the celery stalk.
  • Drain the dill relish.
  • Flake the Crab Classic meat into a large mixing bowl.

Cooking

  • In the mixing bowl with the crab, add the mayonnaise, chopped celery, hard-boiled egg whites, and dill relish.
  • Mix all ingredients thoroughly until a smooth crab salad consistency is reached.
  • Spoon the finished crab salad mixture evenly onto 8 slices of classic white bread.
  • Top with the remaining 8 slices of bread to create 4 complete sandwiches (2 slices of bread per serving).
  • Serve immediately or chill before serving.
