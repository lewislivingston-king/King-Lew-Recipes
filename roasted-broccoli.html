Roasted Broccoli

Description

Nutrition (per serving) : Serving : 1/4th of recipe | Calories : 215kcal | Total Fat : 14g | Total Carbohydrate : 9g | Dietary Fiber : 4g | Protein : 13g | Net Carbs : 5g
A crispy, keto-friendly side dish featuring roasted broccoli florets seasoned with garlic and topped with fat-free mozzarella for a high-protein finish.

Prep Time: 5 minutes

Cook Time: 20 minutes

Total Time: 25 minutes

Ingredients

  • Broccoli Florets "Generic" 8 cups
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil "Generic" 0.25 cup
  • Garlic Powder "Generic" 1 tsp
  • Sea Salt "Generic" 0.5 tsp
  • Black Pepper "Generic" 0.25 tsp
  • Mozzarella Cheese, Fat Free "Generic" 0.5 cup
  • Lemon Juice (Optional) "Generic" 1 tbsp

Cooking Instructions

Preparation

  • Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  • Ensure the broccoli florets are completely dry after washing to ensure they roast rather than steam.

Seasoning

  • In a large bowl, toss the broccoli florets with the olive oil, garlic powder, salt, and pepper until evenly coated.
  • Spread the florets in a single layer on a large baking sheet, ensuring they are not overcrowded.

Roasting

  • Roast on the middle rack for 15 minutes.
  • Remove the tray and sprinkle the fat-free mozzarella cheese over the broccoli.
  • Return to the oven for an additional 5 minutes or until the edges are browned and the cheese is melted.

Finish

  • Remove from the oven and add a squeeze of fresh lemon juice if desired. Serve immediately.
