Taco Salad

Description

Nutrition : Serving : 1 | Calories : 1289kcal | Total Fat : 79g (122%) | Saturated Fat : 18g (78%) | Cholesterol : 160mg (53%) | Sodium : 2557mg (107%) | Total Carbohydrate : 85g (22%) | Dietary Fiber : 20g (80%) | Sugars : 9g | Protein : 89g (178%) | Net Carbs : 65g
A hearty taco salad featuring lean ground sirloin, black beans, chips, avocado, and ranch dressing.

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 20 minutes

Total Time: 35 minutes

Ingredients

  • 100 G 3.5OZ, Fresh Small Avocado
  • 0.50 cup(s), Sliced Olives (Black)
  • 1.50 tbsp, Low Sodium Taco Seasoning
  • 1 cup, Grape tomatoes
  • 8 oz, Lean Ground Sirloin, 90% Lean
  • 28 gram, Fat Free Cheddar
  • 2 tbsp(s), Water
  • 1 bag, Chips (Quest protein chips-taco)
  • 2 tablespoon, Avocado Oil Ranch Dressing
  • 1 tablespoon, Organic Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 0.50 cup, Black Beans
  • 2 oz, Spinach, raw, fresh
  • 0.33 cup, chopped Green onions

Cooking Instructions

Preparation

  • Dice the avocado and chop the grape tomatoes in half.
  • Chop the green onions and slice the black olives.
  • Rinse the raw spinach.
  • Drain and rinse the black beans.

Cooking

  • In a large skillet, brown the 8 oz of Lean Ground Sirloin. Drain any excess fat.
  • Stir in the 1.5 tbsp of Low Sodium Taco Seasoning and 2 tbsp of Water. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  • In a small bowl, whisk together the 1 tbsp of Organic Extra Virgin Olive Oil and 2 tbsp of Avocado Oil Ranch Dressing.
  • In a large bowl, combine the raw spinach, grape tomatoes, sliced olives, black beans, and chopped green onions.
  • Add the seasoned ground sirloin mixture to the vegetables.
  • Top the bowl with the diced avocado and crumbled Fat Free Cheddar.
  • Drizzle the dressing over the top and serve with the bag of protein chips on the side.
