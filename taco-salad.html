Taco Salad (Low Carb)

Description

Nutrition (per serving) : Serving : 1.0 | Calories : 1159 kcal | Total Fat : 79g (121%) | Saturated Fat : 15g (78%) | Cholesterol : 180 mg (60%) | Sodium : 2147 mg (89%) | Total Carbohydrate : 42 g (14%) | Dietary Fiber : 14g (56%) | Sugars : 2g | Protein : 81g (162%)
A high-protein, low-carb rendition of a classic taco salad, featuring lean ground sirloin, fresh greens, and a savory ranch dressing, prepared without black beans to reduce carbohydrates.

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Total Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients (For 1 Serving)

  • Lean Ground Sirloin, Kroger, 8 oz
  • Avacado, Fresh Small, 100 G 3.5OZ
  • Sliced Olives (Black), Great Value, 0.50 cup(s)
  • Low Sodium Taco Seasoning, (N/A), 1.50 tbsp
  • Chips, Quest protein chips-taco, 1 bag
  • Fat Free Cheddar, Kraft, 28 gram
  • Grape tomatoes, (N/A), 1 cup
  • Avacado Oil Ranch Dressing, Primal Kitchen, 2 tablespoon
  • Organic Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Kirkland, 1 tablespoon
  • chopped Green onions, (N/A), 0.33 cup
  • Spinach, raw, fresh, (N/A), 2 oz
  • Water, (N/A), 2 tbsp(s)

Cooking Instructions

  1. In a skillet, brown the **Lean Ground Sirloin**. Drain any excess fat.
  2. Add the **1.50 tbsp Low Sodium Taco Seasoning** and the **2 tbsp(s) Water** to the ground sirloin. Stir and cook until the water has evaporated and the meat is fully seasoned. Set aside to cool slightly.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the **Spinach, raw, fresh**, **Grape tomatoes**, and **chopped Green onions**.
  4. Top the salad mixture with the cooked taco meat, **Sliced Olives**, and **Fat Free Cheddar**.
  5. Chop the **1 bag of Quest Chips** and add them to the top of the salad for crunch.
  6. Dice the **Avacado** and place on top.
  7. In a small bowl, whisk together the **2 tablespoon Avacado Oil Ranch Dressing** and the **1 tablespoon Organic Extra Virgin Olive Oil** to create the dressing.
  8. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and serve.
