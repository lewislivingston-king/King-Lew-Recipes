Chicken Quesadillas

Description

Nutrition : Serving : 1 | Calories : 639kcal | Total Fat : 15g (20%) | Saturated Fat : 1g (5%) | Cholesterol : 172mg (57%) | Sodium : 2443mg (106%) | Total Carbohydrate : 48g (18%) | Dietary Fiber : 30g (107%) | Sugars : 5g | Protein : 102g | Net Carbs : 18g
A high-protein, high-fiber quesadilla made with flour tortillas, fat-free cheese, and fully cooked chicken fajitas. Provides an excellent source of Calcium (92%) and Protein (102g).

Prep Time: 5 minutes

Cook Time: 5 minutes

Total Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 tortilla, Hero, Flour Tortillas
  • 4 oz, Great Value, Shredded Mild Cheddar Cheese, Fat Free
  • 8 oz, John Soules Foods, Chicken Fajitas, Fully Cooked

Cooking Instructions

Preparation

  • Ensure the 8 oz of John Soules Foods Chicken Fajitas are fully cooked and heated through.
  • Grate or measure 4 oz of shredded mild cheddar cheese.

Cooking

  • Place one Hero flour tortilla in a warm skillet.
  • Layer half the shredded cheese and half the chicken fajitas on the tortilla.
  • Top with the second flour tortilla.
  • Cook for 2-3 minutes per side, pressing down lightly, until the cheese is melted and the tortillas are golden brown.
  • Slice into wedges and serve warm.
