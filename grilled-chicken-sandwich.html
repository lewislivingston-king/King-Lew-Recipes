Grilled Chicken Sandwich

Description

Nutrition : Serving : 1 | Calories : 349kcal | Total Fat : 15g (23%) | Saturated Fat : 3g (14%) | Cholesterol : 85mg (28%) | Sodium : 343mg (14%) | Total Carbohydrate : 28g (9%) | Dietary Fiber : 21g (84%) | Sugars : 2g | Protein : 48g (96%) | Net Carbs : 7g
A high-protein sandwich featuring a large grilled chicken breast, provolone cheese, and lean grilled bacon on a high-fiber bun.

Prep Time: 5 minutes

Cook Time: 10 minutes

Total Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 strips, Lean Grilled Bacon
  • 6 oz, Grilled Chicken Breast
  • 0.50 tbs, Olive Mayo
  • 1 Slice (19g), Provolone Cheese Slices (Sargento Reduced Fat)
  • 1 Bun, Hamburger Buns (Hero)

Cooking Instructions

Preparation

  • Cook the 6 oz chicken breast until it is fully grilled and cooked through.
  • Cook the lean grilled bacon until crispy.
  • Toast the hamburger bun lightly, if desired.

Cooking

  • Spread the 0.50 tbs of Olive Mayo on both halves of the toasted bun.
  • Place the 6 oz Grilled Chicken Breast on the bottom bun.
  • Top the chicken with the 1 slice of Provolone Cheese.
  • Place the strip of Lean Grilled Bacon over the cheese.
  • Cover with the top half of the bun and serve immediately.
