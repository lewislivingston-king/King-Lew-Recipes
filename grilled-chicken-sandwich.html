Grilled Chicken Sandwich

Description

Nutrition : Serving : 1 | Calories : 349kcal | Total Fat : 15g (23%) | Saturated Fat : 3g (14%) | Cholesterol : 85mg (28%) | Sodium : 343mg (14%) | Total Carbohydrate : 28g (9%) | Dietary Fiber : 21g (84%) | Sugars : 2g | Protein : 48g (96%) | Net Carbs : 7g
A high-protein sandwich featuring grilled chicken, lean bacon, provolone cheese, and olive oil mayo on a high-fiber bun.

Prep Time: 5 minutes

Cook Time: 5 minutes

Total Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 Bun, Hamburger Buns (Hero)
  • 6 oz, Chicken (Grilled chicken)
  • 1 strips, Lean Grilled Bacon
  • 1 Slice (19g), Provolone Cheese Slices (Sargento Reduced Fat)
  • 0.50 tbs, Olive Mayo (Olive oil mayo)

Cooking Instructions

Preparation

  • Grill or heat the 6 oz chicken and 1 strip of lean grilled bacon.
  • Lightly toast the hamburger bun, if desired.

Assembly

  • Spread 0.50 tbs of olive mayo on the toasted bun.
  • Place the cooked chicken on the bottom bun.
  • Top with the slice of provolone cheese and the grilled bacon strip.
  • Add the top bun and serve warm.
