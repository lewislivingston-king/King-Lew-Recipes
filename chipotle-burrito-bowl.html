Chipotle Burrito Bowl

Servings: 1 • Calories: 1245 kcal

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Super Greens
  • 2.5 oz Fajita Veggies
  • 2 oz Mild Salsa
  • 4 oz Cilantro-Lime Brown Rice
  • 4 oz Grilled Chicken
  • 4 oz Black Beans
  • 2 oz Roasted Corn Salsa
  • 2 oz Pinto Beans
  • 8 oz Guacamole

Preparation

  1. Warm rice, beans, corn salsa, and chicken if desired.
  2. In a large bowl, layer super greens as the base.
  3. Add fajita veggies, rice, black beans, pinto beans, and chicken.
  4. Top with mild salsa, roasted corn salsa, and guacamole.
  5. Serve immediately or chill for 15 minutes to blend flavors.
