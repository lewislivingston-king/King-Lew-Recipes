Low-Carb Chicken Teriyaki Stir-Fry (G Hughes)

Description

Nutrition (per serving) : Serving : 1/6th of recipe | Calories : 558kcal (Approx.) | Total Fat : 17g (22%) | Saturated Fat : 4g (20%) | Total Carbohydrate : 18g (6%) | Dietary Fiber : 6g (21%) | Protein : 83g (Approx.) | Net Carbs : 12g
A high-protein, low-carb chicken teriyaki stir-fry, utilizing the entire bottle of G Hughes Sugar Free Stir Fry Sauce for a rich flavor base and loaded with low-carb vegetables.

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Total Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients (For 6 Servings)

  • 3 lbs (48 oz), Boneless, Skinless Chicken Breast, cubed or sliced
  • 12 oz, G Hughes Sugar Free Stir Fry Sauce (The whole bottle)
  • 3 Tbsp, Avocado or Coconut Oil
  • 2 cups, Sliced Green Cabbage or Coleslaw Mix
  • 2 cups, Sliced Red Bell Pepper and Onion mix
  • 2 cups, Sliced Mushrooms and Zucchini
  • 1 tsp, Ginger Paste or minced fresh ginger
  • 2 Tbsp, Chopped Green Onions (for garnish)

Cooking Instructions

Stir-Fry

  1. Heat 2 Tbsp of oil in a large wok or skillet over medium-high heat. Add the cubed chicken and cook in batches until browned on all sides. Remove the chicken and set aside.
  2. Add the remaining 1 Tbsp of oil to the hot wok. Add the cabbage, bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, and zucchini. Stir-fry for 5-7 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp. Add the ginger paste.
  3. Return the seared chicken to the wok with the vegetables.
  4. Pour in the entire 12 oz bottle of G Hughes Sugar Free Stir Fry Sauce. Toss everything thoroughly to coat.
  5. Bring the mixture to a low simmer and cook for 2-3 minutes, allowing the sauce to reduce slightly and the chicken to finish cooking.
  6. Serve immediately, topped with chopped green onions.
