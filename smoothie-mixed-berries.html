Smoothie Mixed Berries

Description

Nutrition : Serving : 1 | Calories : 184kcal | Total Fat : 7g (11%) | Saturated Fat : 1g (3%) | Cholesterol : 0mg (0%) | Sodium : 2mg (0%) | Total Carbohydrate : 29g (10%) | Dietary Fiber : 10g (41%) | Sugars : 17g | Protein : 5g (10%) | Net Carbs : 19g
A quick and simple high-fiber smoothie made with ground flaxseed, frozen mixed berries, and frozen cherries.

Prep Time: 2 minutes

Cook Time: 0 minutes

Total Time: 2 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp, Ground Flaxseed
  • 4 wt. oz, Frozen Mixed Berries
  • 4 wt. oz, Frozen Cherries

Cooking Instructions

Preparation

  • Gather all ingredients and a blender.
  • If using milk or water, measure the desired amount (typically 1/2 to 1 cup).

Cooking

  • Add the liquid base (milk/water), frozen mixed berries, frozen cherries, and ground flaxseed to the blender.
  • Blend on high speed until completely smooth, pausing to scrape down the sides if necessary.
  • Pour into a glass and serve immediately.
