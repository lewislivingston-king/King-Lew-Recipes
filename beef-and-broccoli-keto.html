Keto Beef & Broccoli Stir-Fry (G Hughes)

Description

Nutrition (per serving) : Serving : 1/6th of recipe | Calories : 551kcal (Approx.) | Total Fat : 29g (37%) | Saturated Fat : 10g (50%) | Total Carbohydrate : 12g (4%) | Dietary Fiber : 4g (14%) | Protein : 68g (Approx.) | Net Carbs : 8g
A high-protein, low-carb rendition of the Chinese classic, using the entire bottle of G Hughes Sugar Free Stir Fry Sauce for a rich, keto-friendly flavor.

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Total Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients (For 6 Servings)

  • 3.5 lbs (56 oz), Flank Steak or Sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 12 oz, G Hughes Sugar Free Stir Fry Sauce
  • 6 cups, Broccoli Florets, fresh or frozen
  • 3 Tbsp, Avocado or Coconut Oil
  • 1 tsp, Ginger Paste or minced fresh ginger
  • (Optional) 2 Tbsp, Liquid Aminos or low-sodium Soy Sauce
  • (Optional) 1 tsp, Xanthan Gum (for thickening)
  • (Optional) 1 tsp, Sesame Seeds (for garnish)

Cooking Instructions

Preparation

  1. Thinly slice the beef against the grain. (If using optional Liquid Aminos, toss beef with 1 Tbsp and set aside.)
  2. Chop broccoli into florets.

Stir-Fry

  1. Heat 1 Tbsp of oil in a large wok or skillet over medium-high heat. Add broccoli and stir-fry for 3-5 minutes until tender-crisp. Remove and set aside.
  2. Add the remaining 2 Tbsp of oil to the hot wok. Add beef in batches (if necessary) and sear for 1-2 minutes per side until browned.
  3. Return all beef and the reserved broccoli to the wok.
  4. Pour in the entire 12 oz bottle of G Hughes Sugar Free Stir Fry Sauce and the 1 tsp of ginger. Stir thoroughly to coat all ingredients.
  5. (Optional Thicken): If desired, mix 1 tsp of Xanthan Gum with 1 Tbsp cold water, pour into the stir-fry, and stir until the sauce thickens.
  6. Serve immediately, garnished with sesame seeds if desired.
